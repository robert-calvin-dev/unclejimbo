About Uncle Jimbo

When people ask me what type of music I mix, I always find that a tough question to answer because there’s always inspiration coming from so many styles that I’ve honestly had a blast learning to mix all kinds of beats from Mid Tempo to Hardstyle.

Gun to my head ? Tech House.

Music has always been an escape for me- a happy place and I think that’s what inevitably drew to learning to mix. I was so lucky to have some of my closest friends like DJ White Rabbit and Jimmy Vu already being experienced DJs because I was able to learn a lot from them.

Best spot to go out dancing in Vancouver ? For me it’s always been Club 816 The World but if the afters aren’t really your thing then Celebrities on Davie for sure is always a blast !
